The Legacy

Jarad Anthony Higgins, known professionally as Juice WRLD, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is considered a leading figure in the emo-rap and SoundCloud rap genres which became mainstream in the mid-late 2010s.

His music touched millions, blending raw emotion with melodic flows. From "Lucid Dreams" to "Legends", his voice resonates with a generation.

"I wanna be more than a millionaire. I wanna change the world."
